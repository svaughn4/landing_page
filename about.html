About Sydney

So you want to learn more about me! I was born in Castro Valley, California back in 2002. However, I was raised around the world due to my mom's job. Our first relocation was to Luanda, Angola where we lived for 3 years. During this time, we were fortunate enough to travel around Europa, Asia, and Africa all of which these experiences strengthened my curiosity, determination, and open-mindedness. Attending an international school as well positioned me in a intellectually, culturally, and socially diverse bubble that deepened my appreciation for difference in thought and values.

In the 6th grade, my mom received an offer in Buenos Aires, Argentina so we moved and resided there for 2 and a half years. There, I became fluent in Spanish: partly because of the international school setting and partly because of the cultural immersion outside of school.
